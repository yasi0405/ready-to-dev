Banking platforms cannot afford ambiguity. They need to absorb spikes, tolerate incidents and remain understandable for the teams evolving them.

## Treat resilience as a product concern

Resilience does not start with multi-region added at the end. It starts with clear decisions on critical dependencies, service levels and acceptable degradation paths.

## Keep the architecture operable

A strong critical architecture is not only scalable. It is observable, testable and legible for teams operating under pressure.

## Standardize without freezing

The practical balance sits between platform standards, repeatable patterns and enough autonomy for product squads.
